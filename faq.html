Is this an official university website?
No, it's a student driven initiative created under guidance of our professor Mr Shrey Khandelwal. The goal is to enhance the student experience, not replace the official site.
What are hostel facilities like?
Hostels are comfortable and well maintained with Wi-Fi, hot water ,laundry and common rooms. There are seperate hostels for boys and girls with 24*7 security.
Is Wi-Fi available throughout the campus?
Yes , the entire campus is Wi-Fi enabled.
What kind of clubs and societies does the university have?
From tech and innovation to drama, dance , debate, photography and music- There is a club for every interest.
Is the academic schedule too hectic or balanced?
It's well structured; Classes are interactive and balanced. So you will learn and still have time to explore campus life.
Do seniors rag juniors?
NOPE! Shoolini has a strict anti-ragging policy but seniors do guide , help and sometimes tease in fun ways.
Is there a medical facility on campus?
Yes, there is a health care centre with qualified doctors and tie ups with mostly hospitals for emergencies.
How's the weather?
Solan weather is mood-swinging:- warm in day; chilly at night. Do keep hoodies , jacket and a blanket ready!
